Built on the latest Android technologies, this application merges performance, security, and user-friendly features to cater to the diverse needs of modern mobile users.

## Features

## How to Use

### Getting Started
1. **Launch the App**: Open the Advance WebView app on your Android device.
2. **Enter a URL**: Type or paste the desired web page URL in the address bar at the top.
3. **Navigate**: Tap the "Go" button to load the page. Use back and forward buttons for easy navigation.
4. **Access Downloads**: Tap the download icon to view ongoing and completed downloads. Manage your downloads with pause, resume, or cancel options.
5. **Settings Customization**: Access the settings menu to customize download paths, toggle dark mode, and adjust other preferences.

### Changing Settings
1. **Open Settings**: Tap the menu icon (three dots) in the top right corner and select "Settings."
2. **Adjust Preferences**: Modify settings such as download location, notifications, and appearance.
3. **Save Changes**: Ensure that you save any changes before exiting the settings menu.

To publish the **Advance WebView** app on the Google Play Store, follow these steps:

1. **Prepare Your App for Release**:

2. **Create a Developer Account**:
   - Visit the [Google Play Console](https://play.google.com/console) and create a developer account. A one-time registration fee applies.

3. **Upload Your App**:
   - Go to the "All Apps" section in the Play Console and click on "Create App."
   - Fill in the required app details, including title, description, category, and content rating.
   - Upload your signed APK or App Bundle.

4. **Prepare Store Listing**:
   - Add high-quality screenshots, a feature graphic, and promotional graphics.
   - Write a compelling app description, including the key features outlined in this README.

5. **Set Up Pricing and Distribution**:
   - Choose whether your app will be free or paid.
   - Select the countries and devices your app will be available on.

6. **Publish Your App**:
   - Review all details and click "Publish." Your app will go through a review process, which may take a few hours to a few days.

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues while using **Advance WebView**, consider the following troubleshooting steps:

- **Network Issues**: Ensure that your device has an active internet connection. Check your network settings and try switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data.
- **Application Crashes**: If the application crashes, clear the app cache and data through the device settings, then restart the app.
- **Download Failures**: Verify that the URLs are valid and that you have sufficient storage space on your device for downloads.
- **JavaScript Not Executing**: Ensure that JavaScript is enabled in the settings. If issues persist, check for any browser-specific restrictions.
